Yes, if you open an account online you will need to print out, fill & sign the below-mentioned documents and send the below forms to the Zerodha Head Office:

(You will have received a copy of your application forms on your registered email address after you e-sign them)

Note: You can download these by either logging into https://signup.ree.com/ OR requesting your sales manager for a copy.
